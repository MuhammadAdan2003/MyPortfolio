About Project

This is a modern e-commerce platform designed for a fictional luxury brand, created to break away from conventional grid-based layouts and deliver a truly cinematic browsing experience. The project leverages GSAP for smooth, scroll-triggered animations, adding a dynamic and immersive feel to every interaction. Built with HTML, JavaScript, and Tailwind CSS, the platform is fully responsive, ensuring flawless performance and visual consistency across all devices and screen sizes. Every element, from product displays to navigation, has been carefully crafted to blend aesthetics with functionality, offering users an engaging and intuitive shopping journey that feels both luxurious and interactive.
