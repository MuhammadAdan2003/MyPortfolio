About Me

Professional Personal

I am a web application developer with nearly two years of professional experience building modern, scalable, and user-focused web applications. I enjoy working across the full development lifecycle, from planning and designing interfaces to implementing backend logic and optimizing performance. My work focuses on writing clean, maintainable code while ensuring applications remain efficient and easy to use. I regularly work with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to create responsive layouts and interactive experiences, and I use GSAP to add smooth animations that enhance usability. I also build dynamic and component based interfaces using React. On the backend, I develop robust applications with PHP and Laravel, handling authentication, business logic, and APIs, while using MySQL to design and manage reliable databases. I continuously improve my skills by learning new techniques, following best practices, and delivering high-quality solutions.


Beyond technical implementation, I place strong emphasis on understanding user needs and translating ideas into practical, well structured solutions. I enjoy breaking down complex requirements into manageable components and ensuring each feature aligns with both business goals and user expectations. Collaboration is an important part of my workflow, and I am comfortable working with designers, developers, and stakeholders to refine ideas and deliver polished results. I focus on building applications that are not only functional but also intuitive, accessible, and visually consistent across devices and platforms.

Outside of my professional work, I dedicate time to activities that help me grow creatively and mentally. I enjoy playing video games, which has strengthened my problem-solving abilities, strategic thinking, and patience over time. Gaming teaches me how to analyze situations, adapt quickly, and improve through consistent effort skills that strongly influence my approach to development challenges. In my free time, I also enjoy reading books, as it allows me to relax while gaining new ideas and perspectives. Alongside these interests, I frequently spend time coding for personal projects, experimentation, and learning. I like exploring new tools, refining my techniques, and pushing my limits. I believe maintaining a balance between work, learning, and personal interests keeps me motivated, focused, and continuously improving.


Beyond my work life, I value experiences that keep my mind active and creative. I often spend time gaming, not just for entertainment but for the way it challenges my decision-making, focus, and adaptability in fast-paced situations. These experiences help me stay calm under pressure and think logically when solving complex problems. I also enjoy reading, which allows me to slow down, reflect, and absorb new ways of thinking from different fields. When I'm not doing either, I usually find myself experimenting with code—testing ideas, building small projects, or learning new technologies out of pure curiosity. This mix of creative outlets and continuous learning helps me stay balanced, motivated, and passionate about growth both personally and professionally.
